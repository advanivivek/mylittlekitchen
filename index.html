3 Pillars of Sustainability

Sustainability must take into consideration the 3 pillars that are the foundation to an unstoppable growth of your restaurant.

ENVIRONMENTAL

How does the way the produce impact the environment, can it be farmed better? Can it use less packaging and less transport?

SOCIAL

The impacts that the produce has on the community & if that product is playing a positive role in rural areas where the jobs are limited.

ECONOMIC

Is the product affordable for the long term of the business. Is it helping in any way to increase or appreciate the bottom line?
